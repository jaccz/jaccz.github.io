# Jacek Czerwonka

Hi! I'm Jacek, a software engineer at Microsoft working on <a href="https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/project/tools-for-software-engineers/">engineering systems</a>. Previously, I worked on Windows and spent time at Microsoft Research.

My interests center on the engineering process and tooling—particularly verification, including <a href="http://citeseerx.ist.psu.edu/viewdoc/download?doi=10.1.1.104.1145&rep=rep1&type=pdf">testing</a>, <a href="https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/wp-content/uploads/2015/05/PID3556473.pdf">code review</a>, security, and compliance. I'm passionate about understanding engineering practices through <a href="https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/6509369/">data</a> and identifying what makes engineers <a href="http://chisel.cs.uvic.ca/pubs/storey-TSE2019.pdf">productive and fulfilled</a>.

Beyond my day job, my team and I publish research papers. I've participated on program committees for several conferences, including ICSE SEIP, ISSRE, ICPC, IWCT, and SANER.
